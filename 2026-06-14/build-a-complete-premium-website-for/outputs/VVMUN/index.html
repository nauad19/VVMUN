About the Conference

A forum for reasoned diplomacy and fearless civic imagination.

Vikram Vichar Model United Nations brings together young delegates for three days of substantive debate, negotiation, policy drafting, and persuasive leadership. Built on the values of intellectual discipline and public service, VVMUN is designed to help participants understand international relations, constitutional thought, and the art of consensus.

From security crises to public health, parliamentary politics to disarmament, every committee is crafted to reward preparation, clarity, collaboration, and the courage to think beyond predictable positions.
